About Us

Mission Statement

Friends of Mill Valley Golf Course is a nonprofit public benefit organization formed to preserve, maintain, and enhance the Mill Valley public golf course as an open-space recreational and environmental resource for the community.

The organization’s activities focus on promoting access to outdoor recreation, supporting environmental stewardship, and encouraging public engagement with this historic and scenic community asset.
