Residents Cup Annual Tournament

The Residents Cup is our annual community golf tournament held each fall since 2021. The event brings together neighbors, families, and local players to celebrate golf while raising funds to support improvements and stewardship at Mill Valley Golf Course.

Yearly Highlights

  • 2021: 48 players | $6,300 raised | Winning neighborhood: East Blithedale
  • 2022: 55 players | $8,450 raised | Winning neighborhood: Homestead Valley
  • 2023: 62 players | $9,975 raised | Winning neighborhood: Tamalpais Valley
  • 2024: 70 players | $12,200 raised | Winning neighborhood: Sunnyside

The event continues to grow each year, fostering local engagement and community support for course preservation.

Clubhouse Beautification Project

We updated and upgraded the front foliage and plantings in front of the golf clubhouse to improve the appearance of the facility and create a more welcoming environment.

Golf Course Improvements

We have funded several projects to improve the player experience, including green renovation, updated signage, tees, and safety enhancements. These improvements help maintain a high-quality course for all ages and skill levels.
