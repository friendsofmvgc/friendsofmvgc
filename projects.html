Residents Cup Annual Tournament

The Residents Cup is our annual community golf tournament held each fall since 2022. The event brings together neighbors, families, and local players to celebrate golf while raising funds to support improvements and stewardship at Mill Valley Golf Course.

Residents Cup Total Impact (2022–2025):
327 players • Over $30,000 raised for Mill Valley Golf Course improvements

Year-by-Year Results

  • 2022: 96 players | $4,600 donated
  • 2023: 91 players | Over $10,000 donated
  • 2024: 78 players | Over $10,000 donated
  • 2025: 62 players | $4,200 donated

The event continues to grow each year, fostering local engagement and community support for course preservation.

Clubhouse Beautification Project

We updated and upgraded the front foliage and plantings in front of the golf clubhouse to improve the appearance of the facility and create a more welcoming environment.

Golf Course Improvements

We have funded several projects to improve the player experience, including green renovation, updated signage, tees, and safety enhancements. These improvements help maintain a high-quality course for all ages and skill levels. We also provide a website to support community fundraising and engagement.
