Mill Valley Residents Cup

The Residents Cup is an annual community golf tournament held at the Mill Valley Golf Course since 2022. The event brings together local players to celebrate the course while raising funds to support improvements and stewardship.

Impact

  • Funds raised support course improvements and beautification
  • Strengthens community connection to the public course
  • Supports long-term preservation efforts
