Volunteer and Support

There are many ways to help the Friends of Mill Valley Golf Course preserve and enhance this historic community resource:

  • Volunteer for events, projects, and course beautification
  • Sponsor a project or community initiative
  • Share our mission with your friends and local networks

We welcome your support in any form. Reach out to learn how you can make a difference.
